About Us

Bella's Shelter is a family ran rescue home for vunarable dogs.
We have been open since May 2020.
We're located in Ballina, Co.Mayo.

Our main principle is every dog deserves a good home.

Our goal is to find a home for our dogs.

Feel free to check the our dogs section for which dogs we currently have.
Be sure to check our socials for regular updates.
